Background

Persona 5 is a turn-based JRPG developed by P-Studio and published by Atlus, released in 2016. Persona 5 Royal is an expanded remake of the original game,
which adds more characters, scenes, and an extra bit of story content. It was released in 2019. It is cited as one of the greatest video games
of all time, with praise for it's gameplay, music, story, and visuals. The player experiences the game through a 17
year old high school student codenamed Joker, who the player is given the opportunity to name, but his "canon" name is Ren Amamiya.
